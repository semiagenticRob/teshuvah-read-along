## Version B — Extended (If More Context Is Helpful)

**Subject:** DavenAlong — Shacharit guidance app, introduced by Rabbi Leban (TJE)

**Body:**

Rabbi Feigenbaum,

Thank you for your time. I was introduced by Rabbi Leban at The Jerusalem Experience — he and the TJE community have been tremendously supportive of what I'm building, and he felt strongly that a conversation with you was worth having.

*What DavenAlong is:*

DavenAlong is a Shacharit-first mobile app designed to remove the two biggest barriers to daily davening: not knowing where you are in the tefillah, and not being able to follow along at pace. The app uses a continuous scroll layout — the entire Shacharit flows as one unbroken experience — with real-time Zmanim integration so the current section is automatically highlighted based on the time of day. Users choose their display (Hebrew only, transliteration, English, or any combination) and their experience level (complete beginner vs. someone returning to davening after time away), and the app adapts accordingly.

Phase 1-2 of development are complete: Shacharit, Mincha, Ma'ariv, and Birkat Hamazon are built with audio. The current development phase is the continuous scroll redesign, which is the feature that will make it genuinely useful for daily practice.

*Where the partnership comes in:*

Right now the app uses transliteration and basic English translations. What's missing is the layer that makes davening meaningful — the structure, the explanations, the warmth of someone who genuinely cares about a person connecting to tefillah for the first time. Rabbi Leban mentioned your siddur specifically because it was designed for exactly this audience: people who want to daven but need guidance to make it click.

I'd love to explore:
- Whether we could license the Feigenbaum siddur text, transliteration, and/or commentary for use in the app
- What a co-branding or endorsement arrangement might look like
- Whether there's a revenue-sharing or royalty structure that makes this work for Mosaica

I'm also open to whatever structure makes the most sense from your side. The goal is to build something genuinely helpful for people who want to connect to Shacharit — and to do it with content that has your name attached to it.

I'd be glad to send a video demo of the current build, share the design specs, or arrange a short call. Whatever is most useful for you.

Thank you again, and yasher koach to you and the TJE team for the warm reception.

Rob Warren
[rob@email.com]
